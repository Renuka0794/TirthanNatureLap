About us

Our home Tirthan Nature Lap is located in one of the hidden valleys of Himachal Pradesh, Tirthan Valley. It gets its name from the river 'tirthan', tributary of beas river. Tirthan is an offbeat destination that has something for everyone. This quiet valley is ideal for trekking, fishing, wildlife watching, and discovering under-explored hill villages. There’s also the option of doing absolutely nothing as you relax among the pine trees in the valley.

Places around us

Choii waterfal

Chhoie Waterfall in Tirthan Valley can be reached through a short trek of 3 km walk that begins from Village Nagini(2km from our stay) which takes about 1 hour. The waterfall is named after local deity Chhoi Mata and locals come to worship the goddess at one of the trees before the waterfall.

Sharchi village

17km away from our stay lies this awe-inspiring village. No other place defines this statement for me as much as Sharchi does. Sharchi, translating to ‘Land of Truth’ is one of the most colorful villages in Himachal Pradesh. Bright colored roofs packed close to each other with greenery and farms all around and the backdrop of gigantic Himalayas – That’s Sharchi for you.

Badasari top

It lies 17 km from our stay, uphill from Sharchi village. The view from the Badasari is breathtaking!! You can see the entire valley, tiny houses scattered across the valley, and the Himalayas, sitting peacefully at a distance add all the beauty that this scenery requires.

Gushaini

Gushaini is a small village in Tirthan Valley. It is prettily located on the banks of the pristine Tirthan River and has lots of greenery. The village has a happy vibe with rustic wooden buildings and smiling kids running in its bylanes. Gushaini is the gateway for treks in Tirthan Valley and is hardly 10 km away from Banjar.

Tirthan River

Just 700 meters away flows the mesmerizing river. It is a 20 km long river that find its away all along the beautiful hamlets. The place being a chilling spot in quaint place of himachal, also offers trout fishing as a adventure activity for tourists.
